Welcome to Explore Nepal

Nepal is a small but beautiful country located between India and China. It is famous around the world for its mountains, temples, culture, and friendly people. Many tourists visit Nepal every year to enjoy trekking, sightseeing, and learning about the traditions of the Nepalese people. This website has been created to give a simple introduction to Nepal and help you explore important information easily. On this website, you can read about famous travel destinations, cultural values, traditional foods, and festivals of Nepal. Whether you are planning a trip or just studying about Nepal, this guide will help you understand the country in a clear and simple way. Enjoy exploring Nepal with us!
